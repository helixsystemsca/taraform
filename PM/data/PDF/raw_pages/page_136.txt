Section 2 – Project Management Performance Domains
2.1.6.9 Close Project or Phase
The Close Project or Phase process involves finalizing all activities related to both successful and
unsuccessful projects, phases, releases, iterations, or contracts. The primary benefits include
archiving project or phase information, completing planned work, and releasing organizational
resources for new projects. The process also includes confirming the extent to which value or the
capability to deliver value has been achieved (see Figure 2-12).
