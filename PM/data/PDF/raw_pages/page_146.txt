2.2.2.3 Define Scope
The objective of this process is to develop a detailed or high-level description of the project,
product, and value expected to be delivered, as well as a quality management plan. This description
may be done at the beginning of the project for predictive approaches or at the beginning of each
iteration for adaptive and hybrid approaches. In predictive approaches, these deliverables should
be structured in the WBS, whereas in adaptive approaches, these deliverables are progressively
defined through a backlog. This process also identifies the quality requirements and standards
for the deliverables, as well as determines how the project will demonstrate that the quality
requirements are met. The key benefit of this process is that it helps ensure that the stakeholders
and project team understand the value that will be delivered through a product, service, or result
(see Figure 2-16).
